Our Story

Built for the
Slow Morning

Noir & Brew opened in Koregaon Park in 2020 with a simple belief — that great coffee deserves great silence. We source micro-lot beans from Coorg and Araku Valley, roast in small batches, and serve every cup with the care it took to grow it.

No rush. No background noise. Just you, your work, and a cup that means something.
